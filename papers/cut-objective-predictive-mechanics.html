Cut-Objective Predictive Mechanics

Boundary response geometry from composable region weights

Version 0.7 · May 21, 2026 · journal submission version

This manuscript develops a finite-dimensional reconstruction result relating composable boundary-conditioned region weights, sharp generators, boundary responses, and Lagrangian response geometry. Rather than starting from a Newtonian state space, Hamiltonian phase space, or action functional, it starts from weights assigned to bounded histories and asks what boundary data are required for fragments to compose consistently across an auxiliary cut.

Under exact gluing, complete auxiliary interface data, a sharp-generator limit, smoothness, and regularity hypotheses, the construction recovers endpoint response covectors and a Lagrangian endpoint response relation, locally symplectic under a standard nondegeneracy condition.
